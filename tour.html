KOLKATA TOURISM

“Calcutta is lovely. “Wherever you place a camera, you get a vision,” said Pradeep Sarkar, a famous writer and director, and he couldn't have been more right. The country's second largest city seems to be a magical town brought to life by a genius novel. The former British capital's old-colonial style buildings bring a certain beauty to the area, making it an absolute delight for the eyes. It's no surprise that many regard the bustling metropolis as the country's intellectual, educational, and creative capital! Take a stroll around North Kolkata to see how the noisy lifestyle of today's crowd works in complete balance against the backdrop of a bygone age. And then there's the food! Don't miss out on a delectably gastronomic experience in Kolkata's alluring food lanes. If the hustle and bustle of city life is starting to bother you, take a seat by one of the picturesque Ghats and let the hot tea make you forget about it!
