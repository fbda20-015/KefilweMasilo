The Largest Tree In The World

If you are asked where the largest tree in the world in terms of area covered is, your response should be The Great Banyan Tree at the Botanical Gardens in Kolkata. Another amazing fact: the tree is over 250 years old.

India has the lowest divorce rate in the world

Divorce occurs at a rate of less than 1% in India. Just 13 marriages out of 1000 end in divorce.The low divorce rate is due to societal pressure; arranged marriages usually last months and involve two families rather than people.

The Oldest Polo Club In The World

In terms of seating capacity, Eden Gardens is the world's third largest cricket ground. After the most well-known MCC, the Calcutta Cricket and Football Club (CCFC) is the world's second oldest cricket and football club.

Second Oldest Cricket & Football Club

Eden Gardens is the world's third largest cricket ground in terms of seating capacity. The Calcutta Cricket and Football Club (CCFC) is the world's second oldest cricket and football club, after the most well-known MCC. Furthermore, the Calcutta Football League has been running since 1898 and is the world's second oldest football league.

Largest Second-Hand Book Market

Kolkata is unquestionably a book lover's paradise.It is home to the country's biggest used book market and the world's second largest. Another explanation why hordes of bibliophiles visit this city all year. National Library is the largest and oldest public library in the world.

Largest Planetarium In Asia!

Birla Planetarium, Asia's oldest, is also the world's second largest. It was built in 1963 and inaugurated by the then-Prime Minister, Jawahar Lal Nehru. Let us also mention that Science City is the largest scientific culture center in the Indian subcontinent.

The Only City To Have Trams & Tanga

The old trams have been redesigned and presented with an AC version, making them much more fun and convenient. Not only are trams available, but Kolkata is the only city in the world where you can travel on hand-pulled rickshaws, which have been operating on city streets since the British occupation.

Oldest And The Only Riverine Port

Since the days of liberation, sea and river ports have expanded and emancipated with the rise in trade and industry. Despite this, few people are aware that the country's only riverine port, known as the Khidderpore Port, is located on the Hooghly River and is also the oldest port in India.

The Jewel Of The East

The Great Eastern Hotel is known as the "Jewel of the East." This grand hotel was established in 1841 and has served most of the world's most famous figures. In addition, The Great Eastern Hotel was Asia's first hotel that was open to the public.
